Our Mission

Here at Cedar Furniture Restoration, our aim is to give you quality furniture. Each piece we restore carries a story, and our goal is to preserve that story. We take in old worn down wooden furniture, and make it functional and beautiful once more for your home. We are committed to sustainable craftsmanship, honest pricing, and work that stands the test of time.
